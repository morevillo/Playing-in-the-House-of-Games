Playing in the House of Games

Thank you for participating in the study! Social Media has rapidly evolved and has now become a major part of our everyday lives. Not a day goes by without us looking through our Facebook accounts even just once. This study aims to find out how much people really know about social media and how it works.

How can you help? By agreeing to take part in the experiment, you will be asked to answer a brief questionnaire about social media and your usage. You will then play a quick game with a randomly assigned player. After playing through the game and asnwering the questionnaire, you will then be personally interviewed about your experiences with social media.

Don't forget to collect your $10 voucher!
