Playing in the House of Games

Stuff here will contain the purpose of the experiment

Thank you for your interest. We are studying the needs of users of community sharing services in New Zealand. (These services allow you to rent, share, or borrow things). The study aims to improve understanding of customers and inform service design.

How can you help? If you agree to take part you will answer a brief questionnaire about your experience with online sharing services, then play a game with a randomly assigned player. This will take around 5 minutes.

You have a chance to win one of twenty $25 Prezzy cards.
